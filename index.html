We are a small family firm of traditional craft bookbinders specialising in the repair, conservation and making of books.

Our customers range from private individuals to libraries and archives.

Our work is as varied as our customers and it includes repair and conservation of books and albums, and the making of new bindings and boxes, in both cloth and leather.

Our bindery has moved from Gaywood Farm in Edenbridge to our home nearby.

If you have a book that needs repair or rebinding, a visit to our bindery can be arranged. Please telephone or email for further information.
